About me

  I received my B.S. degree in Computer Science from the University of Ioannina, Ioannina, Greece (in 1998), an M.Sc. from the same University in 2001 and a Ph.D. degree in Computer Science with title: Global Optimization, methods and software from the University of Ioannina, Ioannina, Greece (in 2006). I have worked on several research programs at Greek universities (University of Patras, University of Crete, University of Ioannina, National Technological and Educational Institute of Epirus). Also, I have worked for several years as a visiting lecturer at universities and Technological Educational Institutes. I have published more than 100 peer-reviewed manuscripts. I have received more than 1900 cross-references to my work. I have served as reviewer for several scientific journals and conferences. My research interests include global optimization, neural networks, genetic algorithms, genetic programming, biomedical signal and image processing. Also, I have worked as a freelancer programmer in the area of mobile applications and I have created more than 60 applications for Symbian, i-phone and Android mobile devices. I hold the position of Professor at the Department of Informatics and Telecommunications, University of Ioannina, Greece.
